Gap cell — the unit of memory

Light flows through the shape, blooms magenta, holds, then the light decays to zero and the cell re-seals into the surface — never a darker hole. At recall the whole board drops to one uniform tone, so the gap pattern survives only in memory.

Uniformity rule: at recall every cell must render in one identical tone. A gap that decayed to a darker hole than its neighbours would betray its shape as negative space — handing the player the answer. The light goes; the hole must go with it.
