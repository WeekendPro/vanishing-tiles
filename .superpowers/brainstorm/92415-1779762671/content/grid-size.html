Which grid size feels right?

Larger grids = more complexity & longer rounds. The shaded cells are already filled; white cells are empty (where tetris pieces go).

8 × 8

64 cells · Compact, fast rounds · Good for mobile · ~2–3 piece gaps per round

8 × 10

80 cells · Sweet spot · Wider feel, rows matter more · Familiar from classic Tetris proportions

10 × 10

100 cells · Spacious, more pieces per round · Longer memory challenge · Better for web; tight on mobile

Dark = filled · Light outlined = empty (piece-shaped gaps)
