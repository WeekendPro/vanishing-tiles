How should manual piece placement work?

This kicks in when your piece selection doesn't perfectly fill the grid. Pick the interaction model that feels best.

→
drag piece → drop onto grid gap

A — Drag & Drop

Drag a piece from your tray, hover over the grid to see a placement preview, drop to place.

Pros

  • Intuitive, spatial
  • Natural on mobile (touch drag)

Cons

  • Harder to implement precisely
  • Frustrating if hit detection is off
1. click piece
→
2. click target cell
piece snaps to grid; invalid placements rejected

B — Click to Select + Click to Place

Click a piece in your tray to "hold" it, then click the grid cell where it should anchor. Piece snaps into position.

Pros

  • Precise, no accidental drops
  • Works equally well on web & mobile
  • Simpler to implement

Cons

  • Two clicks per piece feels slower
  • Less physically satisfying

Either can support piece rotation — worth deciding separately.
