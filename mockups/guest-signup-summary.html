Vanishing Tiles · Design review

Guest sign-up on the game-over summary

A guest finishes a run and lands here. For guests, the run-history chart (“your best run ever”) is irrelevant — nothing is being saved — so that slot becomes a gentle, one-purpose call to action: join the leaderboard. The score, stats, and action row are untouched. Three treatments below, from most reserved to most assertive.

Signed-in players are unaffected — they still see the chart. This variant renders only when isGuest === true.
