low-poly brain · the cheap path

~100 flat triangles, rendered as SVG — the browser zooms/pans it on the GPU with no per-frame JavaScript. Facets light up lobe-by-lobe as you clear levels; the 15 vertices are your levels. Three stages shown. This is about as light as it gets on a phone.

Stage 2 — frontal waking

Stage 8 — two lobes alive

Stage 15 — fully lit

WHY THIS IS CHEAP
  • Static SVG mesh — drawn once, never redrawn.
  • Zoom/pan = one CSS transform on the GPU. No canvas loop.
  • Only the next node animates (CSS keyframes).
  • Lighting a lobe = swapping a few fill classes. Trivial.
