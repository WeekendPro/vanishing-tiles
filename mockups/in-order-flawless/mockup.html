Flawless / In-Order — two-bonus model & central-line messaging

Illustrative mockup in the real Afterglow tokens. Point values are placeholders (per-gap rates TBD). Focus: what fires when, and how it reads on screen.

1 · The model — two independent rewards

FLAWLESS = cleared with no life lost (any order). All modes. IN ORDER = flawless and in reveal sequence. Easy/Medium only.

Because IN ORDER requires flawless, an in-order Easy/Medium clear stacks both bonuses. One lost life forfeits both — no "six misses then a tidy finish" loophole.

2 · The clear payoff — streak owns the center, bonuses lift into the score

At clear, the center line shows STREAK ×N (your ask — the streak, not "CLEAR!"). The two badges + the speed bonus rise one-by-one into the score, extending the game's existing "time → points" lift. This is a flawless in-order Medium clear (both bonuses).
