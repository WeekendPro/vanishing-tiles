GAP CITY · the brain

Three ways to recast the map as a growing brain instead of a subway line. The game trains recall — so the map is a mind being built: every level you clear fires a new neuron and grows the next connection. You start with a single lit cell. Nothing ahead is drawn until you earn it.

A · SYNAPSE BLOOM ★ 14
SYNAPSE BLOOM

Pure abstraction — no brain outline. A dendrite grows from a seed at the bottom, branching upward. Each node = a level; each branch is a memory you wired. Cleared nodes glow in their region's color; the next node pulses white at the growing tip. Beyond it: black. The shape of your brain becomes a record of how far you've recalled. Boldest, most original.

B · LIVING CONNECTOME ★ 14
LIVING CONNECTOME

A faint, recognizable brain silhouette sits in the dark as the territory. Levels are neurons placed across three lobes (your three districts). Cleared neurons are lit and wired to each other; the next neuron pulses at the frontier. Un-earned regions of the brain stay unlit/void. Most legible — closest to the current node-and-line data model, easiest to ship.

C · CONSTELLATION ★ 14
CONSTELLATION RECALL

Memory as a night sky. Levels are stars; clearing one draws the line to the next, sketching a constellation in the rough shape of a brain. Leans into the existing ★ rating (a level's stars literally brighten its star). Softest / most poetic; the brain shape only emerges once you're deep in. A middle path between A's abstraction and B's anatomy.
