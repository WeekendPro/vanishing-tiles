Level flash — centered, minimal, non-interrupting

Fires once when the run first reaches a new Level (Singles → Doubles → Triples → Transformers → Inverts). Quick all-caps flash in the dead center of the grid — the same look as MEMORIZE / RECALL / CLEAR — then gone until the next Level. Doesn't pause the round, doesn't cover a reveal: it rides the breath between rounds. ~900ms total: fade-in (140ms) · hold (560ms) · fade-out (200ms).
