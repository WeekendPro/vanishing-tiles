First-Run Demo Round — “the demo is the game, minus stakes”

A guided two-piece round that runs on the REAL Stagger screen, before the 3·2·1·GO countdown — a timeless space where the loop is learned, then the countdown itself announces that time begins now. No score, no lives, no clock. Mirrors the selected mode (piece colors on Easy, monochrome magenta on Medium/Hard); picks are guided in reveal order, which quietly pre-teaches Hard's IN ORDER rule. Section 2 compares the eye-guidance treatments — they loop, judge them in motion.
