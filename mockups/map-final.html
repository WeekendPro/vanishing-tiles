Transit map of a fictional city. Climb from The Hollows (bottom) to downtown The Grid (top). On a phone this scrolls and auto-centers on your next stop.
GAP CITY ★ 11
STATION STATES
  • Cleared — solid line-color dot + your ★ rating. Tap to replay.
  • Next stop — pulsing white node, the obvious "go here."
  • Ahead (unlocked line) — dimmed, readable but clearly later. (These become the locked nodes when the gating spec lands.)
  • Locked line — dashed dim line + 🔒, not tappable until the prior district opens.
  • ⇄ Interchange — ringed node where one line hands off to the next.
PALETTE
  • ● Hollows = neon cyan
  • ● Stacks = neon magenta
  • ● Grid = neon green (yellow reserved for ★)
CHROME

GAP CITY wordmark + total ★. Scanline overlay over the whole map. Tap a station → existing Level Detail modal (PLAY / PR / stars). No streak counter — no such data exists, so we don't invent it.
